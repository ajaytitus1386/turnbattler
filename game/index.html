Instructions

This is a turn based battler that uses a turn timer displayed near your character. Your enemy will attack repeatedly on each turn. On your turn, you choose between your available actions. You and the enemy have separate timers based on your stats.

Some actions such as picking an enemy for an attack or choosing an action have keybinds specified near them.


This section will show details during the fight.
Remember not to spam the action buttons as each turn takes a certain time to complete!
